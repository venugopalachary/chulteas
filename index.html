Our Dishes

Chuletas

At Chuletas, we blend tradition with taste, creating appetizers that redefine homemade goodness. Our commitment to quality ensures every bite is a celebration of flavours. We create each flavour in our kitchen with hand-blended spices and real ingredients you can see. Our passion is making simple food that tastes amazing. Try our cutlets and cheese balls. We think you’ll agree that they are the best appetizers ever!

Everything we do centres around high-quality ingredients and delicious food. This doesn’t stop at the food we make, we want to spread out the love for great food, made from the heart. For three decades, a cherished family recipe has brought delight to our home. Now, Chuletas is proud to bring this age-old secret to your table. Our frozen appetizers, made with the same love and passion that inspired the original recipe, promise an authentic taste of tradition in every bite. Handmade with fresh ingredients, our offerings are a testament to the warmth of homemade goodness. With zero preservatives and a shelf life of 45 days, indulge in the essence of our heritage, now served for you to savour.

Vision

"To Preserve Tradition, One Bite at a Time. We envision a world where the authentic flavours of homemade snacks, passed down through generations, are savoured and appreciated globally. Our commitment lies in crafting frozen treats that echo the essence of home, connecting people through the shared love for genuine, traditional, and delicious experiences."

Mission

"At Chuletas, our mission is to bring the heart-warming taste of authentic, homemade snacks to every household. We dedicate ourselves to meticulously curating recipes treasured across generations, using only the finest and freshest ingredients. Through innovation, quality, and a deep respect for culinary heritage, we strive to provide frozen treats that evoke the warmth of home-cooked meals. We are dedicated to delighting our customers, fostering community, and preserving the rich tapestry of flavours that define our cultural heritage."
